About Us:

We are pleased to introduce ourselves as an SSI Unit
Registered with Director of Industries,Odisha as
Designers, Engineers & Manufacturers having an
Experience in the field of Steel Structural Fabrication
& equipment manufacturing.

The structural & equipments are for various Steel Plants
Chemical Plants, Cement Plant, Sponge Iron Plants etc.
We work as per drawing & specification provided along with
The purchase order. We are also capable of designing of the
Work from the GA drawing provided by the party.

Our scope of work include –

Bending, Machining, Casting and Fabrication of :

Equipments Electrode Column,Roller Table,Tundish Car,
Ladle Car etc.
Heavy structural Fabricated Column, Roof Beams, Roof
Plates, Auxiliary Girders, Chord Bracing, Trusses etc.

Technological structural such as Main Frame for Housing
Platforms, Hoppers, Bins, Silos Pedestrals etc.

Machining job such as – Rollers,Flanges, Bearing Blocks,Shaft
Foundation Bolts etc.

Also we Manufacture :

Manufacturing & Supply of Technological Structures and Electric Panels.

Manufacture, Assembly, shop testing Inspection, surface preparation, painting Packing, loading of Rellining Platform.

Manufacture of Protection Plate for Ladle Wrecking Stand. Fabrication, Assly. Surface Preparation for CCP and Tundish Suspension Stand for Billet Caster. Storage Stand for Mould & Tundish Stopper Rod. Cooling chamber & steam Exhaust System Suction Ducts & pressure ducts upto Roof.
Manufacture & maintenance of Stair Case, Hand Drill, Column Bracing Laced Purlin & Purlin, Technological equipment for Column Bracing, Chimney Spiral etc. as per specification & drawing

Manufacture, Assembly, Operational & Functional Test
Without load to the maximum extend possible, Inspection
Of surface preparation & painting, packing ,forwarding
loading & supply of Strand BBBL Caster.

Assembly stand for bloom mould insert, beam blank
Insert, storage stands for mould & tundish stopper rod.
